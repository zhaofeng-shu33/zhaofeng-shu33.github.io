As we can see generally, Yang Li was much more hard-working than the boss Shao-Lun Huang. She came very late to dormitory sometimes. She is also a little bit "push" to her students and trainees.

Today there is one unpleasant thing happening between Yang Li and me, also between professor Yang and Fei Ma. Recently, both of us were submitting some paper and we asked Yang as co-author to review the article. The problem is that we do not give the final submitted version before we submitted the paper to the target system. Yang Li was a little bit angry about this kind of thing, out of our expectation. Fei Ma was in his dorm while I was at the lab when this thing happened. Fei called me through wechat to discuss this problem. We thought Yang was changing and it was different to get associated with the current person.
