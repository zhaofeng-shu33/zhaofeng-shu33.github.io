写于2009年 5月12日 周二
English Translation is written on August 18th 2014.

（1）

今天对中国，是不平凡的一天，汶川地震一周年祭；对我的八年级10班，更不是普通的一个下午，因为，在练歌的过程中，发生了许许多多的事情，气人恼人也好，感人动人也罢，被时间的潮水冲刷过后，便只有对她的敬意了。 ­

下午刚到校，就发现张晓羽的位置底下竟放着一包草莓，红色塑料袋包裹着洗净的草莓，小巧玲珑的草莓，有许多呢，味道如何呢？这时我才想起原来是上个星期生病的周老师送的礼物，曾亲自嘱咐我们一定要发给同学们吃，尝尝乡土的味道，没有温室的味道，化肥的味道。­

草莓于是就在下午大课间发下去了，张晓羽一边小心翼翼地抓出一把放在同学的位上，一边介绍说这是语文老师送的。拿出来的草莓可没看上去鲜亮，吃进去才知道软软地已经浸泡得变了味，同学们私下里你推我让，不愿意吃，馋嘴的吃了一个也直摇头。原本还象征着师爱的草莓现在却成了可以相互赠送的“礼物”。也是，爱的保质期又有多久？我沉重地接过张晓羽递上来的草莓，沉重地咀嚼着乡土的味道。

发草莓并不是设想中那样的温馨与感动。 ­

下午真巧全自习，她们几个人一商量要练歌。一面是作业，一面是音乐，两事难全。我对唱歌也不大热心，和同学不约而同地选择写字，“都‘站起来’唱！”张晓羽的“号召”我们不得不听，尽管如此，歌声也好比政治老师的呓语，有气无力。

第二节课要下楼排队形，更是乱套。男生打，闹，音乐老师和张晓羽 说好说歹 也无济于事。时间分秒流逝，队形越排越乱，要排练的动作进行也不顺。烈日下，我们很难站稳与坚持，他们大声“呻吟”着，张晓羽的嗓音也沧桑了许多。好歹清唱了两遍歌，每每到一半，该高的地方成了低谷，无果而终，累的够呛。 ­

第三节课张晓羽有事出去，训练继续，刘月把歌声开着，下面不听指挥，结果引来级部主任强力将练歌课改成了并不安静的自习。

第四节课刘月和尚雅晴这两个暴力倾向的女生一齐上阵，一面大声叫喊“都‘站起来’”，一面到下面去拽人。可惜大家仍眷恋着越写越多的作业。一肚子的怒火无处寻的尚雅晴突然看到女生同胞竟也都坐着写作业，真气坏了，找她的同位夏霜开刀，喊了她几次夏霜都不理会，尚雅晴便大声地朝她也是朝所有同学叫喊道“还练不练哪！”说着随手捡起汽水瓶就扔过去，“咣当”一声响亮而清脆。夏霜也把笔一摔，狠狠地回击道：“练练，练什么练！再练就把嗓子练毁了，明天怎么比赛！”于是两人就吵起来，空气中的火药味呛人。 班里女生纷纷来劝，没用——幸好张晓羽来得及时 。夏霜愤愤地出了教室，一行人追了出去，她们回来时，都不说话。可怕的沉默中，我们不情愿地放下手中的题。半节课已过，我们又有什么进步呢？今天天色暗的竟如此早，而我们也都是如此疲惫，想到练歌中的辛酸，想到我们还能   “站起来”吗？ 张晓羽站在讲台上，用被阳光灼伤的手臂支着讲桌。这时我们仿佛听得见七班的嘹亮的歌声，他们和我们一样，选的歌也是《站起来》，这时正在操场上紧张地排练。

(1)

Today is a special day for China, as it was one year mourning for the huge earthquake which happened in WenChuan. But today is more special for our Class Ten, as it showed her touching virtue during our process of singing rehearsal.

Her name was Dawn Feather, under whose seat I found a packet of strawberry, which was given by our Chinese teacher, Ms Round. This is a special present not only because of the giver’s fragile state after a terrible accident but the taste of rural regions, borne by the small and dainty strawberry.

However, thestrawberry was not as popular as imagined, partly because of the deteriorated taste after delay of deliver and partly because of our fickle feelings towards someone who teaches us what Chinese is.

So there was no strangeness when a dipped strawberry was pushed fro and back. It became a second hand “gift”, which disappointed Dawn Feather quite a lot when she dispensed the precious strawberry which represented love of teacher.

It happened that we could rehearse our chorus the whole afternoon since there was no regular class then. The organizers turned out to be all female roles, Moon, Fair Sunny, Summer Frost and the general composer --- Dawn Feather.

But our homework tended to be in proportion to our self-studying time. We have no mood to sing without finishing homework first . So we just turned deaf ears to the instruction of Fair Sunny. But we cannot obey the directions of Dawn Feather publicly. Under her continuous encouragement, we merely made some sound less loudly than mosquitoes made.

The first class passed with little progress. Then in the second class we were called downstairs to arrange the formation. The beautiful music teacher was requested by Dawn Feather to help us. But some naughty boys always went against the instructions. Time passed second by second, minute after minute. But the more eager Dawn Feather tried to put us in order, the more turbulent our formation was. There was no shade between the hot sun and us, and we felt hard to stick to standing firmly and singing our parts. Anyhow we managed to proceed our song two times. But the effect seemed minor.

In the third class we were allowed to do homework but soon in the fourth class we were again forced to stand up and sing loudly by Moon and Fair Sunny, the infamous two violent-tended female organizers (Dawn Feather was absent). Moon even rushed into the group of us to drag her acquaintances to cooperate with them. But we were still attached to writing our increasing homework including one organizer, Summer Frost. She was spotted by the angry-proceeding Fair Sunny, who could not bear any more. After several shout to her desk mate, Summer Frost received no reply, Fair Sunny exclaimed towards the whole classmates in her whole strength, “Don’t you all refuse to rehearse !? ” At the same time she brutally threw a bottle of drinking towards Summer Frost. Fortunately the bottle hit the ground with a sharp and crisp sound. But the collision between the two tomboys burned out. Summer Frosts lammed her pencil away and cruelly retorted, ”Rehearsal! You only know rehearsal! If we continue to rehearse, our voices will be ruined totally! How can we win the contest tomorrow!” The quarrel expanded and classroom air was laden with a smell of gunpowder. Girls tried to split them one by one without success.

Fortunately, Dawn Feather came back in time. By her interposition Summer Frost left the classroom with irritation. A couple of girls followed her. When they came back, they kept silence. In the frightening silence, we had to put down our pens against our willingness. Another half class was wasted in such a way of practicing but we had little progress. It got dark so early today and we were so tired. Thinking of the bitterness mixed into the rehearsal, we wondered whether we could “standup”. At this time Dawn Feather stood on the platform, with elbows supporting chins. At this time I seemed to hear the loud and clear singing of Class Seven. They sang the same song, “StandUp” as ours. They were rehearsing on the playground at the moment.

（2）

“我们的对手，就在下面，练着呢。”……“我们是最后一个出场的，可偏偏七班是倒数第二个。要知道，我们都唱同一首歌，如果我们和他们的歌声差不多，这根本比不出来的，我们很可能就输了，除非我们比他们强   一百倍。可是，可是我们真的    比七班好吗？就这个状态，练了三节课，有一点效果吗？”她的声音，很轻很柔，却掷地有声。

讲台上的她就忽然提起了语文老师，“其实我一直想抽一节课，给大家说说咱们语文老师的病情的，前天作为学生代表，我们去探望，我真的非常——非常震撼吧……” ­

我在心里叹了口气，先是语文老师病了，再就是数学老师三天没来上课了，听说他是家里有点事，现在这个班级又由谁来撑起呢？ ­

她接着说，“你们知道吗，语文老师她不是摔伤了腿，是腰啊，整个一个人，就侧躺在床上，不能动弹；可是她却把笔记本靠在墙上，坚持给我们写日志。那种辛劳，又 有谁可以做到？你们知道老师每上一节课，都要准备多长时间？学生和老师，哪个更幸福呢？” ­

我仿佛觉得，此时张晓羽的声音就像语文老师一样，她的眼睛也像周老师一样，她 不就是班级的中流砥柱吗？

“后面的几排同学，你们可知道，老师一直都很关心你们，即使别的老师 不闻不问你们，她也从来 就没放弃过你们。她特意向我和王涵询问你们的情况，我们很坚决地回答，你们都很好，即使换了语文老师，也一定能像以前一样好。想想吧，你们做到了吗，辜负了我和王涵的对老师的答复不重要，只是令老师伤心哪，你们！” ­

前天的一幕一幕，又都重现在我眼前，因为老师的嘱托，也有我在听哪！

“你们总是抱怨，包括我曾经——周老师总是布置很多作业，相信大家从初一到现在，有时10点之前不熄灯都是在做语文作业，甚至要拿出甚于其他作业几倍的时间完成；你们还在抱怨，老师讲课文，尤其是文言文，总是很细致，很唠叨地讲语法，讲内容，我们都有点烦了，干嘛讲那么细？可当我们换了老师后才发现，那些都是多么宝贵啊。不是我有意批评现在的语文老师，我只是实事求是，他确实没有周老师做的好，连他自已也承认。尽管周老师训过你们，罚过你们，可毕竟两年了的师生情，又怎能轻易割舍掉，  又怎能轻易忘记？” ­

“也许，期末之前，老师是回不来了，可她一直在争取，一定能带我们初三的语文！我们就这样去辜负，去辜负老师的一片赤诚吗？” ­

…… ­

泪水划过我的脸颊。

“其实我以前，一直是个不爱学习的孩子，可自那次探望，无论是老师，还是老师的父母，都讲——成绩，就是对老师最大的安慰，不只是学习成绩，更是我们这次的比赛成绩，这是为生病的老师交上一份满意的答卷啊。” ­

“要用自己的努力回报老师，无论老师在还是不在，我们都要做的更好，更好……” ­

­­    张晓羽调开了音乐。这次，同学们不约而同地“站起来”了。 ­

“站起来，我的爱牵着山脉……”此时铃声突然打响了，我们仍放声高歌——“多少，梦想的主宰，勇敢和我一起赢回真爱……”伴奏突然中断了，我们一心一意地继续，“站起来终点没了，起点也会在！” ­

我们用声嘶力竭的声音昭示——一定可以战胜七班！ 我们一定可以！­

最后张晓羽还不忘交代：“我希望…每个同学都能写一份小纸条，就像现在墙上挂着的梦想纸条。写上你们对老师的最真诚的祝福，——多少字都可以。” ­

之后，她低低地说了一句，“同学们放学吧。” ­

(2)

“Our rival is, just, below. They are, just, rehearsing.”… “We are the last to sing tomorrow, but Class Seven was just the last second. We must know, we sing the same song with Class Seven. If we sing similarly with them, we can never overwhelm them then---we will probably fail. We probably will fail, unless we are one hundred times better than them. But, but are we really better than Class Seven? Relying on our current state now, which we have rehearsed for three and a half classes, are we really better than Class Seven?” Dawn Feather said bitterly, clearly and softly but with hardness.

She referred to our Chinese teacher sentimentally, “To tell the truth, I really wanted to tell you the condition of our Chinese teacher. The day before yesterday as one of student representative I went to visit her. I was really, really, (She's lost in words), really touched.

I signed at heart, thinking of recent chaos in our class. With the head and math teacher onbusiness for more than three days, with our reverend Chinese teacher seriousinjured, and with those naughty and mischievous boys more frantic, who cansupport this class now?

She went on, “Do you know that our Chinese teacher, who broke her waist rather than her leg. The whole person, lying in bed, can not move away. But she made full use of those painful times to read The Historical Records and kept diary and educational thoughts . Is there anyone who can achieve such a hard-working attitude? Do you know how much time our Chinese teacher spent on preparing a Chinese class? Do you think teachers are much happier than us because they can…but…”

She choked herself for a while. At this moment I felt her voice were like our Chinese teacher; her eyes were like our Chinese teacher; Whatever to say, she was definitely the pillar supporting our Class Ten.

“Students on the back rows, do you know that our Chinese teacher is always caring about you, even if other teachers will desert you if they taught this infamous disordered class---but she never, never gives up on you. During that visiting hour she asked Water Elegancy and me in particular for your conditions. We both answered firmly that, you were all very well. I also promised that even now another Chinese teacher taught us, they still kept progress every day. Think of yourself, guys, do you accomplish these expectations? It’s trivial for you to let Water Elegancy and me down, but—But you made our reverend Chinese teacher grieved, you guys!”

Those pictures of visiting hour ran through my mind, as I was also on that spot, hearing the entrustment of our Chinese teacher with my own ears.

“You, including me, are always complaining that our Chinese teacher assigned much homework every day. I believe that, if we did not finish homework after 10pm from Junior One up to now, it was mostly likely that we were doing Chinese homework. Sometimes we even spent several times finishing Chinese homework as more as getting other subjects’ done. You, including I, are always complaining that our Chinese teacher were too much meticulous at texts, especially at classical Chinese essays, at which she repeated many times at words, grammar, contents. We are somehow impatient at her meticulousness and wonder whether necessary to go into such detail. But after we are taught by another Chinese teacher, how precious those meticulousness was! I never intended to criticize our present male Chinese teacher. I only said the truth that he did actually not better than our reverend Chinese teacher. It was admitted even by himself. My dear friends, though our reverend Chinese teacher once punished you, criticized you, how to cut ourselves from the nearly two-year teacher-student affinity, how to forget those unforgettable times we spent together towards a colorful future ?”

“It’s most likely that our reverend Chinese teacher were unable to come back to us before final exams. But she strived to teach us in Junior Three, if we were still classmates. Considering all our Chinese teacher did for us, how can we fail to live up to her entrustment, fail to live up to her love for us?”

……

Tears shed frommy eyes and dropped silently on my homework paper.

“To tell the truth, I was not a study-lover before. But after that visit, I knew that grades, only grades were great comfort for both teachers and parents. Not only study grades, but our competition grade. This is also a satisfying test paper handed to our ailing Chinese teacher.”

“We are supposed to reward our teacher with our own efforts, no matter where our Chinese teacher is --- I believe we can achieve better, and much better…”

Dawn Feather turned on the music, and this time all classmates stood up coincidently.

(lyric) “Standup, my love links the mountain…” Suddenly the ring rang, we went on, “How many, practitioner of dreams, bravely win the true love with…” The song accompaniment suddenly stopped, we went on without notice, “Stand up even if without the finishing line, we know the starting line lies there forever.”

At long last before we left, Dawn Feather entrusted us to prepare a blessing sentence for our ailing Chinese teacher. “I wish all the sentences are from our hearts.” She said softly with hardness.

(The end)

第二天：歌咏比赛。­

第二天下午比赛，最后一个出场，  2个小时的等待，我们没喝一滴水。尽管大家口渴到不行，可声音依旧很大。“跑过的精彩依然在，Heyya，泪水是胜利感慨……”  我们没忘记张晓羽的嘱托，喊出来了比赛的最强音：­

——五十六个民族心连心，­

筑起钢铁意志长城。­

中国，站起来； 世界，站起来！ ­

与此同时，齐刷刷的横幅，闪了出来，高高地飘扬着，直至结束。我们一直很卖力，以至于后来九班同学说我们跑调了，好像是的。­

但我们也好像拿了第一，彻底地把对手比下去了。­

大家都很高兴呢。­

歌咏比赛，无论是七年级，还是八年级，我们都是Number 1呵！ ­
